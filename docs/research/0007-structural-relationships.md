# Pass 7: Structural relationships

Percepts require explicit, reproducible relationships before any future concept association. Recording edges rather than calculating them only on demand preserves capability provenance, evidence references, deterministic inspection, and a traceable experimental surface. These structural edges remain distinct from lineage: lineage explains ancestry; a relationship describes current exact geometry.

Strict bounds predicates prevent centroid-based overstatement. Equal normalized coordinate patterns are translation-normalized coordinate equality, not recognition; translation vectors do not assert movement, action, or causality. Pairwise analysis is registration ordered and capped at 64 percepts, so this small playground remains a structural microscope rather than an unbounded search system. Semantic association, interpretation, hypotheses, reasoning, reflection, learning, and ARC solving remain deliberately deferred.
